# 第四十三章：参考书目——深入学习的资源

---

## 经典教材

1. **Hartshorne, R.** *Algebraic Geometry*. Springer, 1977.
2. **Mumford, D.** *The Red Book of Varieties and Schemes*. Springer, 1999.
3. **Shafarevich, I. R.** *Basic Algebraic Geometry*. Springer, 1977.

## 交换代数

4. **Atiyah, M. F., MacDonald, I. G.** *Introduction to Commutative Algebra*. Addison-Wesley, 1969.
5. **Eisenbud, D.** *Commutative Algebra with a View Toward Algebraic Geometry*. Springer, 1995.

## 数论与算术几何

6. **Silverman, J. H.** *The Arithmetic of Elliptic Curves*. Springer, 2009.
7. **Cornell, G., Silverman, J. H.** *Arithmetic Geometry*. Springer, 1986.

## 模形式与朗兰兹

8. **Diamond, F., Shurman, J.** *A First Course in Modular Forms*. Springer, 2005.
